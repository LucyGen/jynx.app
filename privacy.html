Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2025

Introduction

Welcome to Jynx. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This privacy policy will inform you about how we look after your personal data when you visit our website/application and tell you about your privacy rights and how the law protects you.

The Data We Collect

We collect and process the following data:

  • Email Addresses: We collect your email address when you provide it through our application.

How We Use Your Data

We use your email address for the following purposes:

  1. To send you notifications about activity on the platform
  2. To collect feedback about your experience with Jynx
  3. To maintain our client database
  4. To send newsletters and updates about our services

Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not share your email address with any third parties except as required by law.

Data Security

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your personal information from unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction.

Data Retention

We will retain your email address for as long as legally permitted under French law, or until you request its deletion.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Request access to your personal data
  • Request correction of your personal data
  • Request deletion of your personal data
  • Object to processing of your personal data
  • Request restriction of processing your personal data
  • Request transfer of your personal data

If you wish to exercise any of these rights, please contact us by email.

Data Deletion Requests

If you would like to have your data deleted from our systems, please send an email to privacy@jynx.app with the subject line "Data Deletion Request" and include the email address you used to register with Jynx.

Cookies and Tracking

  • We do not use cookies on our platform.
  • We use Google Analytics to collect anonymous information about how visitors use our website. Google Analytics uses technologies that do not identify you personally. The information generated is transmitted to and stored by Google on servers that may be located outside your country of residence.

International Transfers

As Jynx operates worldwide, your information may be transferred to and processed in countries other than the one in which you reside. These countries may have data protection laws that are different from those in your country.

Age Restrictions

Jynx is a social media platform (similar to Tinder and Discord) and is intended for users who are 13 years or older. We do not knowingly collect data from anyone under this age.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our privacy policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new privacy policy on this page and sending you an email notification.

Contact Us

If you have any questions about this privacy policy or our data practices, please contact us at:

  • Email: privacy@jynx.app
  • Address: 123 Gaming Street, 75001 Paris, France
